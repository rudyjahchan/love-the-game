How do Games Engage?

_Rudy says_

Rules

A restriction of freedom that give structure to thoughts and variables to derive decisions.
Once a rule is broken the validity of the competition is void.

_Leo says_

Goals & Objectives

They provide clear milestones that we work towards, giving us a reason to play.

_Leo says_

Outcomes & Feedback

Ways to win but more importantly ways to lose.
They provide feedback as competition, discovery, and other value.

_Leo says_

Conflict / Competition / Challenge / Opposition

The push or pull of resources, and their manipulation by people to obtain the goals.

_Leo says_

Representation or Story

Relates it to the players' interests, while also allowing imagination to be released in a controlled environment.
